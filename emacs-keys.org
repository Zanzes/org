C = *Control*
M = *Alt*
S = *Win*

* Avy
** Go to char
   M-s
   
* Config
** Reload
   C-c r

** Open lzconf.org
   C-c e

* Org
** Load #+ line
1. Put cursor at end of line
2. C-c C-c

** Checlists
*** Create new item
M-Shift-return

*** Check
C-c C-c

*** Display check status
**** [x/x]
1. write [/] in header
2. C-c C-c on it

**** [x%]
1. write [%] in header
2. C-c C-c on it

** Dating
*** Open calender
C-c C-s

*** TODO Schedule event
- C-c C-s
  + Must be on headline

*** Add deadline
- C-c C-d
  + Must be on headline

** Mark with todo keyword
C-c C-t

* Movement
** Switch/Next window
   C-x o
